Hello there! I'm a second-year undergraduate student based in the San Francisco Bay Area. My main interests lie in Statistics, Economics, and Film.

I consider the visual arts my guilty pleasure; photography and film in its entirety are two ways to truly express oneself. In here, you will see a collection of recent films that I have past watched, as well as a portfolio of the past photos I have recently taken.

I am also deeply vested in the realm of numbers, where I enjoy pondering about the infinite stacks of data that we face today. The projects that I participated in are articulated in its respective section. I hope to decipher what that data really means, and make an impact on our world, someday.
